Savage Worlds Web Tools

This web app is a collection of tools to help make your preperation and gameplay in Savage Worlds pen and paper role playing game run much more smoothly. I'm doing my best to make sure that all tools look good from phone-sized screens to full desktop boxes, but I'm targeting iPad sized screens as an optimum.

Flexible Dice Roller

Roll some Savage Dice. This little tool should be able to handle pretty much any combo you throw at it. It'll even handle damage rolling and wound calculation for you if you wish.

Raise Calculator

Do you get confused in the middle of a game how many wounds someone gets with a certain number? We undertstand! The numbers sometimes get lost in the story. Here's something that'll help.

Extras Database

A tool to help you quickly get your bad-guy face on. This part is feature-complete, but I still have quite a bit of data entry to complete.

Character Creator

This is a rudimentary Character Creator for the Savage Worlds pen and paper role playing game. This section is quickly approaching usability, only a gear is missing from novice functionality, advancements are current work in progress.

Starship Creator

This is the Starship Creator. You're able to load, save, import and export your creations on your local devices.

Vehicle Creator

This is the Vehicle Creator. You're able to load, save, import and export your creations on your local devices.

Walker Creator

This is the Walker Creator. You're able to load, save, import and export your creations on your local devices.

Power Armor Creator

This is the Power Armor Creator. You're able to load, save, import and export your creations on your local devices.

Mass Battles

A quick Mass Combat calculator. Dice rolls and all.

World Maker

The Science Fiction Companion gives us a Fast, Furious and Fun! way of creating a planet and possibly a civilization. This tool allow for both random random and selection of world traits.

Robot Creator

This is the Robot Creator. You're able to load, save, import and export your creations on your local devices.

Space Travel Time

A close-enough-to-accurate calculator for finding travel and lightspeed communication times in a solar system scale setting.
